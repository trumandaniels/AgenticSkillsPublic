# Pieter Levels

## Context

- known for: solo-founder portfolio, worldview-driven products, automation-heavy operations
- edge: simple products tied to the same audience and worldview

## Signature Moves

- let multiple products exist only when they reinforce the same audience loop
- use simplicity and automation as leverage for a one-person business

## Use This Founder When

- the founder has multiple products that may or may not belong together
- the business needs founder-light systems without adding headcount quickly

## Do Not Cargo-Cult

- do not imitate the quantity of projects without the shared audience loop
- do not assume simplicity means lack of discipline
