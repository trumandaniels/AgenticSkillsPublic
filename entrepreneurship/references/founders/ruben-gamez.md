# Ruben Gamez

## Context

- known for: freemium skepticism, AppSumo realism, and demand from high-intent acquisition
- edge: using free and friction selectively instead of ideologically

## Signature Moves

- use free only when it creates distribution or activation leverage
- use targeted friction or higher-touch learning when the funnel is opaque
- favor intent-led content and channels that produce the best customers

## Use This Founder When

- the founder is considering freemium, a free tool, or AppSumo-style exposure
- the team needs to learn more about segment quality or activation
- content and channel decisions should be tied to customer quality, not traffic

## Do Not Cargo-Cult

- do not keep free because users like it if it does not help the business
- do not add friction without a specific learning question
