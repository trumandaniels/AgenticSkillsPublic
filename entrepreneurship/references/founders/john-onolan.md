## Context

- known for: Ghost, open source plus hosted SaaS
- edge: independence positioning, pricing for customer quality, convenience-layer monetization

## Signature Moves

- raise prices to improve customer quality, not just revenue
- translate technical advantages into buyer outcomes
- monetize open source through convenience, reliability, and managed scale

## Use This Founder When

- low prices are attracting bad-fit customers
- open source or architecture needs a clearer commercial story
- buyers care about independence, control, or reduced platform risk

## Do Not Cargo-Cult

- do not raise prices without real value and trust
- do not lead with technical identity language when the buyer cares about outcomes
