# Arvid Kahl

## Context

- company: FeedbackPanda, a bootstrapped teacher-productivity SaaS later sold
- edge: community-first bootstrapping and deep niche participation
- recurring lesson: trust and language quality come from living inside the niche before extracting a product

## Signature Moves

- join the target community before trying to sell into it
- learn repeated pains and exact language from inside the group
- treat independence and customer closeness as strategic advantages, not constraints

## Use This Founder When

- the founder has niche access but not yet a clear problem or ICP
- trust and domain fluency matter to the business

## Do Not Cargo-Cult

- do not mistake follower count for embeddedness
- do not build for adjacent founders when the buyers live elsewhere
