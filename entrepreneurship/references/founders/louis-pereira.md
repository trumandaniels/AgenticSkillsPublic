# Louis Pereira

## Context

- company: AudioPen, built after many smaller public experiments
- edge: using adjacent small bets and a relevant public audience to spot pull
- recurring lesson: disproportionate response to a tiny experiment is often the signal to zoom in

## Signature Moves

- ship many small adjacent products and watch for disproportionate pull
- build in public so a relevant audience sees the experiments early
- treat Product Hunt as amplification rather than discovery
- use lifetime deals intentionally and early, not permanently
- prefer direct async customer feedback over noisy always-on chat

## Use This Founder When

- the founder has several adjacent ideas
- the business is deciding where to launch first
- early validation matters more than polished growth theater
- the team wants better customer feedback without promising instant response

## Do Not Cargo-Cult

- do not hop randomly across markets
- do not rely on launch platforms to create demand from zero
- do not create documentation to excuse a confusing core flow
