# Intent-Led Content And SEO

Use when:
- the founder wants to use content, SEO, docs, or free tools for acquisition
- the business serves buyers already searching for a workflow, template, comparison, or outcome

Decision types:
- acquisition
- SEO
- content strategy
- docs as distribution

What must be true:
- the buyer already searches for the problem or output
- the team can create assets close to the buying moment

Why it works:
- high-intent assets compound when they answer a real buyer job instead of broadcasting generic thought leadership

What to copy:
- build one high-intent asset such as a template, tutorial, comparison page, free utility, or deep guide
- start from buyer pain and search intent
- refresh what already attracts good customers

What not to copy:
- blogging broadly for vanity traffic
- choosing topics for volume instead of intent

Failure modes:
- attracting the wrong audience
- publishing disconnected content that does not bridge into the product

What to measure:
- source-to-activation quality
- search query intent
- assisted conversions
- retained customers by content source
